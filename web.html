What is Web Designing?

Web designing is the process of creating the Front End of the website. This process includes a website’s Mockup creation, Webpage layout, Styling, Functionalities & Responsiveness.

Who is Web Designer?

Web designer is the one who has the job of styling and designing of the websites to create stunning look, view and feel of the websites.
