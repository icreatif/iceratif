Graphic Designing

What is Graphic Designing?

Graphic designing is where we create graphics for mass media in various form such as print , web and digital. It includes Logo designing, Business Identity creation, Icons for web etc.

Who is Graphic Designer?

Graphic designer is who can sketch fresh ideas of graphics on paper. Then, he/she turn those sketches into digital graphics.
